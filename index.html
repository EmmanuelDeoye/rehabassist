Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers about pricing, plans, and what you get.

What's included in the Free plan?

The Free plan gives you access to core tools with monthly usage limits:

• 5 assignments per month
• 3 ROM analyses per month (Full Joint Assessment only)
• 3 gait analyses per month (video not saved)
• 1 project per month (Chapter 1 generation only)
• Basic documentation tools
• Report and Documentation modes

No credit card required — just sign up and start using the tools.

What do I get with the Student plan?

The Student plan is designed for healthcare students who need more:

• Unlimited assignments generation
• 10 ROM analyses per month (Isolate Joint + Full Assessment)
• 10 gait analyses per month with video auto-save
• Unlimited projects with all 5 chapters
• AI Supervisor for guidance on your work
• Custom outlines for reports and documentation
• Moderate output detail level (3500 tokens)

Perfect for coursework, clinical placements, and research projects.

Why should I upgrade to Pro?

The Pro plan removes all limits and adds premium features:

• Unlimited everything — no caps on any tool
• Presentation Maker with export options
• Flexible AI mode for more natural, detailed output
• Detailed output (5000 tokens) for comprehensive results
• Priority AI processing — faster generation times
• Unlimited cloud storage for all your work
• Team collaboration features

Built for practicing clinicians, researchers, and power users who need the full toolkit.

How does billing work? Can I pay in my local currency?

Yes! Prices are automatically converted to your local currency based on your location. We support:

• Paystack — Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa (Card, Bank, USSD)
• Flutterwave — Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Cameroon, and more
• Google Pay — US, UK, India, Canada, Australia, and Europe

Choose between monthly or yearly billing. Yearly plans save you 20%. You can cancel anytime from your account settings — no long-term contracts.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time:

• Upgrading takes effect immediately — you get instant access to new features.
• Downgrading takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
• You won't lose any saved work when switching plans.
• Your usage counters reset according to your new plan's limits.

Manage everything from your subscription page.

What happens when I hit my monthly limit on the Free plan?

When you reach your monthly limit:

• You'll see a friendly notification letting you know.
• The tool will show an upgrade prompt with your remaining days until reset.
• Your limits reset automatically every 30 days from when you first used the tool.
• You can still access all your saved work and history.

To continue working immediately, upgrade to Student or Pro for unlimited access. Your progress is never lost.

Is my data safe? What about patient confidentiality?

Your privacy is our priority:

• All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
• We never share, sell, or train AI models on your uploaded content.
• Your work is stored securely on Firebase with strict access controls.
• We recommend de-identifying patient information before uploading.
• You can delete your data anytime from your account.

Important: rehablix tools are assistive decision support — they are not diagnostic instruments. Every AI-generated output should be reviewed by a qualified professional before clinical use.

How accurate is the AI? Should I trust the results?

Our AI is designed to be a clinical co-pilot, not a replacement for professional judgment:

• ROM analyses use multi-frame visual assessment validated against clinical measurements.
• Gait analyses extract 5 frames per video for pattern recognition.
• Assignments and projects use multi-pass generation for natural, human-like output.
• All content passes through a humanization engine to avoid AI detection patterns.
• Pro users get flexible AI mode for more nuanced results.

Always review AI-generated content before submission or clinical use. The AI is a starting point — your expertise makes it complete.

Can I use rehablix on my phone?

Yes! rehablix is fully mobile-responsive and works on any modern browser:

• All tools work on smartphones and tablets.
• Camera-based tools (ROM, Gait) use your device camera.
• You can install rehablix as a PWA (Progressive Web App) for quick access.
• Look for the "Install App" button or use your browser's Add to Home Screen option.
• An internet connection is required for AI processing.

Dedicated iOS and Android apps are on our roadmap for offline capabilities.

Who is rehablix built for?

We built rehablix specifically for rehabilitation professionals and students:

• Physiotherapists — ROM analysis, gait monitoring, documentation
• Occupational Therapists — Assessment formats, progress notes
• Students — Assignments, projects, study tools, exam prep
• Researchers — Literature reviews, methodology, data presentation
• Clinicians in orthopaedics, neurology, pediatrics, and psychiatry

Whether you're in a teaching hospital, private clinic, or university, our tools are designed to fit into your workflow — not replace it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers about pricing, privacy, and what's coming next.

Is rehablix really free?

Yes — for now. During our early access phase, all tools are completely free so we can gather feedback and improve the experience. In the coming months, we'll introduce a free tier with core tools and a Pro subscription that unlocks advanced AI features, unlimited usage, cloud storage, and team collaboration. Early adopters will receive special launch pricing.

Which features will become paid?

We're still finalizing the lineup, but here's our current plan:

Free tier (stays free):
• Assessment Format Generator
• Standardized Tools (limited downloads)
• Documentation Assistant (basic transcription)

Pro tier (upcoming):
• ROM Analyzer with video analysis
• Gait Monitor with full biomechanics reports
• Presentation Maker with export options
• Unlimited cloud storage & history
• Priority AI processing
• Team dashboards & collaboration

Nothing is set in stone — we're listening to user feedback before locking anything down.

How is my patient data protected?

Your data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES‑256). We never share, sell, or train AI models on your uploaded content. For logged‑in users, data is stored securely on Firebase with strict access controls.

Important: We recommend de‑identifying all patient information before uploading. A dedicated HIPAA‑compliant Enterprise tier with BAA support is on our roadmap for institutional subscribers. Until then, rehablix is best used with anonymized data.

Are the AI results reliable enough for clinical use?

rehablix tools are assistive decision support — they are not standalone diagnostic instruments and should never replace clinical judgment. Every output must be reviewed by a qualified rehabilitation professional.

We continuously train our models on real‑world mobility data to improve accuracy. Upcoming Pro features will include evidence‑based confidence scores and citation‑backed recommendations so you can verify the reasoning behind every suggestion.

Can I use the tools offline or on my phone?

Currently, rehablix requires an internet connection because AI processing runs in the cloud. The web app is fully responsive and works on any modern smartphone or tablet browser.

Offline‑capable mobile apps for iOS and Android are part of our Pro subscription roadmap. These will allow on‑device processing for tools like ROM Analyzer and Documentation Assistant when you're working in areas with limited connectivity.

Who is rehablix built for?

We built rehablix specifically for members of the rehabilitation team in psychiatry, pediatrics and orthopaedics. Whether you work in a private clinic, hospital, sports setting, academic research, or home health, our tools are designed to fit into your existing workflow, not replace it.

Students and educators are also welcome. If you're teaching or studying rehabilitation, many of our tools (like Standardized Tools and Presentation Maker) are built with you in mind.

What subscription plans are coming and when?

We're targeting mid‑2025 for our first subscription launch. Here's the planned structure:

🆓 Free Tier — Core tools with monthly usage limits
⭐ Pro Plan (~$19–29/month) — All tools, unlimited usage, cloud storage, priority processing
🏢 Enterprise Plan (custom pricing) — HIPAA compliance, SSO, admin dashboard, dedicated support, BAA

Early adopters who create an account now will receive a significant discount when paid plans launch. We'll email registered users with details before any changes take effect — no surprises, no auto‑charges without consent.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. When subscriptions launch, you'll be able to cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no long‑term contracts or hidden fees. If you cancel, you'll retain access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period, then your account will gracefully downgrade to the free tier — you won't lose any data.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Paystack (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa), Flutterwave (multiple African countries), and Google Pay (international). The available options depend on your country.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel from your account settings anytime. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Free plan gives you access to basic features with monthly usage limits. No credit card required.

Will I be charged in my local currency?

Yes. Prices are displayed and charged in your local currency. We use current exchange rates to ensure fair pricing.
